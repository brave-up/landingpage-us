BRAVE UP! is a software that detects and prevents cases of bullying and cyberbullying in schools. Runner-up in the Turner MBA Impact Investing Network and Training (MIINT) of Harvard University. Our mission is to support counselors by helping them better understand the social relationships of students and their everyday problems and concerns, allowing for data-driven interventions to take place.

The Challenge

  • Bullying disrupts the education of half of the young people worldwide. (UNICEF)
  • 75% of victims of school violence never report it.
  • Internet relationships have an impact on the school.
  • Isolation during the pandemic has made social interaction more challenging.
  • Scarce information for making decisions

Why BRAVE UP?

  • Resources

    Access content, videos and activities targeted at understanding the problems that students face in their everyday lives.

  • BRAVE UP! Mode

    Anonymous report Channel where members of the school community can directly message the counseling team.

  • BRAVE UP! Data

    Students participate in a short survey, which makes it possible to generate sociograms in real time, and therefore prevent risk situations and detect cases of bullying and cyberbullying.
